Our church is embarking on an exciting building improvement project to create a more comfortable and welcoming environment for worship. Below are the key improvements we will be making:

1. Replacing Church Pews with Padded Chairs

We will be removing the current pews and replacing them with individual padded chairs. This will provide more flexibility in seating arrangements and increase overall comfort during services.

40%

2. Repairing the Water-Damaged Vaulted Ceiling

Our beautiful vaulted ceiling has suffered from water damage over the years. We will be making necessary repairs to restore its structural integrity and aesthetic appeal, ensuring a safe and beautiful worship space for years to come.

20%

3. Installing New Carpet Squares

The current flooring will be replaced with durable and attractive carpet squares. This new flooring will not only enhance the appearance of the sanctuary but also reduce noise and provide added comfort.

40%

We are grateful for the support of our church community in making these much-needed improvements possible. Stay tuned for updates on the progress of the project!
